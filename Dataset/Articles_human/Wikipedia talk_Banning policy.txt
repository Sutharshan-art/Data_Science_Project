IBANs do indeed give an advantage for the first person to edit a particular page, which is one reason that the enwiki community is often hesitant to impose them. 2-way IBANs work well in cases of mutual agreement between experienced editors, and 1-way IBANs work okay if the subject doesn't edit very often.
